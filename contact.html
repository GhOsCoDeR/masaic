Frequently Asked Questions

How can I purchase your products?

You can purchase our products through our online store or visit our physical locations in the Eastern Afram Plains and Kwahu. We also ship internationally to select countries.

Do you offer farm tours?

Yes, we offer guided farm tours where you can learn about our sustainable farming practices. Please contact us to schedule a visit. Group tours are available on weekdays.

How can I partner with Mosaic Grove?

We welcome partnerships with organizations and individuals who share our vision. Please fill out the contact form with your proposal and our team will get back to you within 48 hours.

Do you offer training programs?

Yes, we offer various training programs in sustainable farming and entrepreneurship. Check our Impact page for more details or contact us for upcoming schedule and registration details.
