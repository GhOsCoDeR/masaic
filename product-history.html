Origins

The Cashew Legacy

Native to Brazil, cashews were first introduced to East Africa in the 16th century. Our premium cashews are sourced from sustainable farms in Tanzania, where the perfect climate and soil conditions create the ideal environment for cultivation.

Cultivation

Sustainable Farming

Our partner farmers use traditional organic farming methods, ensuring the highest quality cashews while preserving the environment. Each tree is carefully tended to, and harvesting is done by hand to maintain the integrity of the nuts.

Processing

Artisanal Processing

Our cashews undergo careful processing in our state-of-the-art facility, where traditional methods meet modern food safety standards. Each nut is inspected for quality before being carefully roasted to perfection.

Heritage

Ancient Super-Tuber

Tiger nuts have been cultivated for thousands of years, dating back to ancient Egypt. These nutrient-rich tubers were a staple food source and were even found in ancient Egyptian tombs.

Farming

Traditional Cultivation

Our tiger nuts are grown in the fertile soils of Ghana, where traditional farming methods have been passed down through generations. The unique climate and soil composition contribute to their exceptional quality and taste.

Innovation

Modern Applications

We've revolutionized the use of tiger nuts by creating innovative products like our signature tiger nut milk, flour, and snacks, while maintaining their traditional nutritional benefits.

Origins

The Dragon's Tale

Dragon fruit, originally from Central America, has found a perfect growing environment in our East African farms. Our cultivation program began as part of our initiative to introduce sustainable exotic fruits to the region.

Innovation

A Kenyan Innovation

Named after their developer, Mr. Wambugu, these unique apples represent African agricultural innovation. They are specially adapted to grow in tropical climates, making them perfect for sustainable local farming.
